Data Analyst

Responsibilities:
- Collect, clean, and analyze datasets to derive actionable insights
- Build dashboards and visualizations to communicate findings
- Collaborate with product and business teams to support data-driven decisions

Requirements:
- Proficiency in SQL and Excel
- Strong knowledge of data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI, or Matplotlib/Seaborn)
- Understanding of statistics and data analysis techniques
- Basic experience with Python or R is a plus
